# Guardian Shield 🛡️

**Guardian Shield** is an advanced, discreet personal safety application tailored for modern threats. Developed with stealth and reliability in mind, this app equips you with a powerful suite of emergency response features designed to help you stay safe—even in offline scenarios.

---

## 🔥 Core Features

- **Discreet Emergency Triggers**
  - **Hardware SOS:** Simultaneously squeeze **Volume Up + Volume Down** to silently trigger an SOS without even taking the phone out of your pocket or unlocking it.
  - **Gesture SOS:** Perform a discreet 3-finger swipe down anywhere inside the app to silently launch a background panic signal.
  - **Manual SOS:** A large, instantly accessible, glowing red SOS button right on the main dashboard for immediate alerts.

- **Offline Emergency Mode (Network Independent)**
  - *When cellular data is dead, Guardian Shield still defends you locally.*
  - **Max-Volume Siren:** Instantly overtakes Android’s audio channels, forcibly blasting a loud siren at 100% volume even if your phone is set to "Silent" or "Do Not Disturb".
  - **Strobe Flashlight:** Continuously and rapidly flashes your camera's LED to disorient attackers and signal for visual help in the dark.
  - **Local Audio Recording:** Automatically begins recording background audio as legal evidence, permanently saved directly to your local device.

- **Ghost Mode (App Disguise)**
  - Hide the true purpose of the app from prying eyes. Guardian Shield can seamlessly camouflage its home screen app icon and name in your phone's drawer to look exactly like a boring native "Calculator" or "My Notes" app. 

- **Dead Man's Switch**
  - A proactive safety timer. Once activated, you must tap a continuous "I'm Safe" check-in before the countdown expires. If you fail to respond (e.g., if your phone is snatched or you are incapacitated), Guardian Shield automates a full SOS broadcast instantly on your behalf.

- **Digital Breadcrumbs & GPS Tracking**
  - **Real-Time Streaming:** Once an SOS is triggered, your live GPS coordinates are continuously updated and broadcast to your emergency contacts via your backend cloud server.
  - **Critical Battery Protocol:** If your phone’s battery drops into critical range (below 5%), the app automatically sends a final "bread-crumb" snapshot—uploading your last known location safely before the device dies completely.

- **Secure Cloud Evidence Vault**
  - Automatically encrypts your emergency logs and audio clips, securely syncing them to a private Supabase backend for permanent, tamper-proof safekeeping that an attacker cannot delete.
